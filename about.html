why we should care about what we consume:

  • many of the diseases we face today (obesity, cancer, heart disease, high blood pressure, diabetes, etc.) are caused/affected by the food we eat.

  • processed foods are dependent on synthetic fertilizers and chemical pesticides, which produce hormones to help promote the speed of growth. eating these foods is essentially consuming additives.

  • organic farming practices reduce pollution, conserve water, reduce soil erosion, increase soil fertility, and use less energy.
